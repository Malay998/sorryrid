What Changes
Now
because apologies only matter when actions change too

I'll communicate softer

Not every feeling needs to come out sharply. Not every frustration needs instant reaction. I want to learn how to explain things without making you feel attacked in the process.

I'll stop trying to "win"

Arguments aren't supposed to become competitions. And somewhere in the middle of defending myself, I forgot we were supposed to be on the same side.

I'll listen before assuming

Sometimes I react to the tone instead of understanding the emotion behind it. I want to become better at hearing what you actually mean, not just what was typed on the screen.

I'll be more patient

Love isn't just about good moments. It's also about handling misunderstandings properly. And I know patience is something I still need to improve.

✦

I'm not promising perfection

Because realistically, we'll probably still annoy each other sometimes. We'll still misunderstand things. We'll still have moments where emotions become messy.

But what I can promise is this:

I'll try harder to protect your feelings, instead of only protecting my ego.

And I'll keep learning how to love you in a way that feels safe, not exhausting.

💕 “Real love isn't never messing up. It's being willing to grow after you do.” 💕
